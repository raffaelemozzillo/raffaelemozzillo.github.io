My research

I am an aerospace engineer, near to catch PhD at Politecnico di Torino, Italy. I will have my PhD defense in the coming few months
My research focuses on technologies and methodologies to improve CubeSats performances, in detail ADCS, failure detection through neural network, and Assembly Integration and Testing/Verification as means to reach that goal.

ADCS

Algorithms to improve attitude determination for CubeSats. Some of them hav been implemented on a CubeSat that will be in space on April 2016

Failure Detection

Neural networks for detecting sensor failures in small satellites. Implementations in Matlab/Simulink

AIT/V

Experience on assembly, integration, functional and evironmental verifications. Hundreds of hours spent in lab!
