StarSim

Simulator technology for space systems

Design of a simulator technology for In-The-Loop simulations.
Allows the perform testing and verification of software and hardware during different phases of the product cycle. From Algorithm-In-The-Loop, where only the functional behaviour is tested, to progressively reaching the Hardware-In-The-Loop where flight hardware is inserted in the simulation. In this case, the simulator will provide the hardware the inputs as they would appear during the mission. The simulator allows also to cross-compile code to be inserted in the flight hardware.
Repository: bitbucket.






Ground Control Station

CubeSat Team GCS for our satellites

Complete design of PoliTo CubeSat Team Ground Control Station software.
Modular and data-driven, developed to be used to track our satellites, and to help during Hardware In The Loop simulations.
